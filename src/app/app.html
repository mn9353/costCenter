Performance Hub — Cost Management Guide

Overview

This dashboard serves as a high-density, read-only cost monitoring utility for Crown Manufacturing. It allows cost controllers, CFOs, and finance directors to review historical actuals, forecast future demands, compare revised forecast scenarios, and perform quick visual variance checks in real time.

Revised Forecast Scenarios (RFC)

In corporate finance, RFC stands for Revised Forecast Scenario (or Re-Forecast). A forecast is never static; it is updated periodically throughout the fiscal year as actual spending data becomes available:

RFC 1 (Q1 Forecast Update)

Compiled after the first quarter closes. It integrates Q1 actual values (Jan–Mar) with revised forecast estimations for the remaining three quarters (Apr–Dec).

RFC 2 (Q2 Forecast Update)

Mid-year forecast update compiled after the second quarter. It incorporates the first half's actual numbers (Jan–Jun) and updates second-half estimations.

RFC 3 (Q3 Forecast Update)

Updated forecast compiled after the third quarter closes. It uses 9 months of historical actuals (Jan–Sep) and refines estimations for Q4 (Oct–Dec).

RFC 4 (Q4 Forecast Update)

The final re-forecast scenario updated just before the fiscal close. It represents the final, most accurate forecast projection before yearly audits begin.

Time Horizons: H1 and H2

Budget cycles are split into half-years to measure performance trends and implement mid-course adjustments:

  • H1 (First Half): Covers the first 6 months of the fiscal year (January 1st through June 30th). H1 actual spending data forms the basis for the RFC 2 mid-year adjustments.
  • H2 (Second Half): Covers the final 6 months of the fiscal year (July 1st through December 31st). Financial managers monitor H2 projections to implement cost-saving plans and keep expenditures within limits.

Implemented Features (Demo Reference Sheet)

These features showcase the application's responsiveness and enterprise preparation during your client demo:

  • Draggable Columns: Click and drag any forecast scenario column header horizontally. Teal indicator borders will appear to preview placement when rearranging.
  • Dynamic Scenarios Modal: Click "+ Add Scenario" to trigger a modal. Newly generated columns immediately populate with trend-accurate mock values.
  • Zebra Layout & Frozen Columns: Frozen left metadata pane on desktops. On mobile screens, only the Account column remains frozen to fit smaller viewports.
  • Dynamic Filtering & Summaries: Changing choices in the Site or Team dropdowns filters child rows and instantly recalculates parent categories and variance columns on the fly.
  • Visual Variance Badges: Color-coded pills (Green for favorable savings, Red for unfavorable overspends) showing differences between actuals and forecast projections.
